About Me

Beth graduated from the University of Sheffield in 2011 having studied Structural Engineering and Architecture. Since then Beth has worked in architectural and engineering practices in Sheffield, Gloucestershire and now Bristol, working on projects ranging from domestic extensions to new build sports halls and high security NHS facilities. In 2019, Beth became a Chartered Engineer with the Institution of Civil Engineers.

Beth is also a Certified PassivHaus Designer and local group leader for the AECB, and is passionate about making low-energy building available to all.
